About the Artist


Born and raised in Minnesota, now located in Carson City, Nevada, Matthew Camelon lives with
his wife and three children. Through his backyard shop Matt creates concrete sculpures and artwork
specializing in foax boa effects. Birdbaths, railings, yard decorations, and even fountains are just a few
things that he has made through this concrete sculpting.


He began his work as an apprentice under master sculpter Matthew G. Welter at Timeless Sculpters. After a few years, Matt
left his work there and continued on his own starting with wood carving and later transitioning into his present day concrete work.

Please visit the contact page for any inquiries regarding work.
